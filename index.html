About

Join the MetaNurses Alliance

The MetaNurses are ordinary professionals that had to step up to the challenge of caring for patients and saving lives with limited resources and support during the Global Covid-19 Pandemic. During this extraordinary call-to-duty we didn’t see ourselves as heroes. We sacrificed time, family, and our mental and physical health for the good of others and our countries. This special commemorative collection pays tribute to the women and men in the nursing profession that worked tirelessly during the pandemic by documenting this mission on the Solana blockchain for all of eternity.

The MetaNurses Alliance is an exclusive collection of Nurses working on the Solana Blockchain with only 5,555 NFTs in existence. Your MetaNurse NFT gives you VIP access to special events & discounts and unlocks holders-only utility that will grow over time.

Your MetaNurse NFT serves as your digital identity and verification in this members only alliance.

MetaNurses believe in the power of blockchain technology to build the nursing community in order to innovate and inspire positive change for the nursing profession. MetaNurses will be a global lifestyle brand aimed at intersecting blockchain and NFT technologies with the nursing profession. We seek to build a loyal and diverse community that aims to drive real impact for our nurses.
